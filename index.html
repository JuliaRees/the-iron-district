Experience industrial living at its finest, where simplicity meets urban edge in the heart of Berlin.

Exposed brick walls create a striking backdrop, showcasing the building's history and adding a touch of rugged character. Large windows allow natural light to flood the space, accentuating the raw beauty of the surroundings.

Explore the neighborhood's industrial charm as you venture out to nearby cafes, eclectic shops, and acclaimed restaurants. Immerse yourself in Berlin's vibrant atmosphere, with iconic landmarks and attractions just a stone's throw away.

Equipped with essential amenities, our apartments cater to your practical needs. Stay connected with high-speed Wi-Fi, relax in the no-frills living spaces, and take advantage of the fully-equipped kitchens featuring modern appliances.
